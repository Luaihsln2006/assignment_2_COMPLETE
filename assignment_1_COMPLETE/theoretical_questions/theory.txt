
Differences between Linked Lists

Singly Linked List:
- Uses: Simple data storage, stacks, queues
- Pros: Less memory, simple
- Cons: One direction traversal only

Doubly Linked List:
- Uses: Undo/Redo, navigation systems
- Pros: Two direction traversal
- Cons: Extra memory

Circular Linked List:
- Uses: CPU scheduling, playlists
- Pros: No NULL, continuous loop
- Cons: Complex implementation
